## Repository Purpose

The purpose of this repository is to provide clear, structured evidence of:

- Business problem analysis  
- Requirements elicitation and documentation  
- Business process and operating model design  
- Governance and change impact analysis  
- Conceptual data modelling and decision traceability  

Each artefact addresses a distinct aspect of ICT business analysis and together they form a coherent, non-duplicative body of evidence.

## Artefact Relationship

The artefacts are designed to be read together:

- **Artefact 1** defines *what* the system must do  
- **Artefact 2** defines *how* the business operates with the system  
- **Artefact 3** defines *how decisions and interactions are structured as data*  

This structure avoids duplication while demonstrating end-to-end ICT business analysis practice.
